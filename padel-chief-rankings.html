UX Flow
1
Default Rankings
Official Padel · All Time · Sri Lanka. Top 3 shown as a gold/silver/bronze podium — tap to expand stats. Ranks 4+ are a compact list. Your position (Marco) pinned above.
2
Switch Sport
Toggle between Padel and Pickleball — ratings are sport-specific so the list is entirely separate. Active sport is highlighted in its brand color.
3
Official vs Community
Official rankings are computed by the platform algorithm. Community rankings weight peer-reported results and local tournament data more heavily.
4
Open Filters
Sheet slides up for Time Period, Gender, and Location (Country → City). The tune badge shows how many filters deviate from their defaults.
Rating System
SOS = Strength of Schedule. +/− = rating change since last update. Contrib. = total contribution points earned. Confidence reflects how many matches the algorithm has to work with — more matches = higher confidence.
